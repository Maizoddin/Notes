Directives :

1] Structural Directives :
   These directives modifies the structure of the DOM by adding or removing elements.
   a] *ngIf
   b] *ngFor
   c] [ngSwitch]

2] Attribute Directives :
   These directives does not change the structure of the DOM but modify the existing element or component.
   a] [ngStyle]
   b] [ngClass]
